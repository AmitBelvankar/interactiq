01 · Why

The gap between content and learning

Authors know their subject. Instructional designers know pedagogy. Today there's nothing in between — until now.

01
Wrong interactions = poor learning

Most authors default to MCQs for everything — regardless of content type. A process becomes a quiz. A decision scenario becomes a fact check. Learners disengage and retention drops.

70% of courses use only MCQ
02
No intelligent guidance exists

Every authoring tool gives authors a blank canvas and a library of widgets. None tell you which interaction fits your content. The decision is left entirely to the author's intuition.

Zero AI guidance in any tool today
03
Instructional designers are scarce

Hiring qualified IDs is expensive and slow. Enterprise teams building at scale cannot embed an expert in every authoring decision. AI is the only way to democratise instructional design expertise.

$80K–$120K avg. ID salary
InteractIQ fills the gap between content creation and learning design — delivering expert-level interaction recommendations at zero marginal cost per analysis.
